Top 5 Rural Destinations in India

Escape the hustle and bustle of city life and explore India's most peaceful rural getaways.

1. Mawlynnong, Meghalaya

Known as "Asia’s Cleanest Village", Mawlynnong offers scenic beauty, Living Root Bridges, and panoramic views of Bangladesh.

2. Khimsar, Rajasthan

Experience the golden dunes, camel safaris, and Rajasthani heritage in this desert oasis.

3. Ziro Valley, Arunachal Pradesh

A UNESCO nominee with rolling green hills, pine forests, and the famous Ziro Music Festival.

4. Kutch Villages, Gujarat

Home to the vast white salt desert and rich traditional handicrafts.

5. Spiti Valley, Himachal Pradesh

A high-altitude Himalayan wonderland with ancient monasteries and breathtaking landscapes.
